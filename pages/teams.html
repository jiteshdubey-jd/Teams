Dr. B.K. Mishra

-Chairperson TCET NSS UNIT

"TCET emphasizes holistic student development through platforms like the National Service Scheme (NSS) catering to undergraduate college students since its establishment in 2005., encouraging volunteer engagement in socio-economic activities. The unit's proactive planning and weekly projects reflect exemplary discipline. Volunteers, future engineers, address societal issues and foster a sense of responsibility. A proposed magazine amplifies their voices for social awareness. Acknowledging the initiative's importance, appreciation is extended to the NSS UNIT and its leaders for this impactful effort, fostering pride and congratulations for the dedicated volunteers."

Programme officers

Dr. Satish Singh

-Programme Officer

FROM THE PROGRAMME OFFICER’S DESK

Proudly introducing the debut TCET NSS UNIT Magazine! As a dedicated Program Officer, I've witnessed students joining in their second year, driven to enact change. Over two years, these volunteers engage in impactful projects, evolving significantly by the program's end. From recruitment initiatives to Blood Donation Camps, Society Outreach, and electricity conservation awareness, each experience leaves an indelible mark. As a seasoned leader, I've seen students grow through these ventures, embodying the core NSS values of discipline, punctuality, and respect. Congratulations to all volunteers for their selfless dedication and profound growth within the community
